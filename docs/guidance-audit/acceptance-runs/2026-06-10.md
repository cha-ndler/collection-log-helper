## Findings (to be filed as issues; fix separately, never in this PR)

1. **Auto-advance abandons a drop-less grind (re-activate guard dead in live path).**
   `GuidanceOverlayCoordinator.onSequenceComplete` calls `deactivateGuidance()` (which
   `OverlayDeactivator.deactivate` → `guidanceSequencer.stopSequence()` → `activeSource=null`)
   BEFORE invoking `handleSequenceComplete`, whose not-unlocked branch then reads a null
   source and falls through to ranked advance. Intended behavior (its own javadoc): re-activate
   the same source until the slot unlocks. Live repro x2: 13:45:08 and 13:46:20 — Giant Mole
   kill with no clog drop → rolled to next Top Pick, no "re-activating" line. Unit test passes
   because it wires the supplier directly. Fix: capture the source before deactivating. The fix
   should also (a) skip re-activation when the source has no missing items left (operator's
   fully-completed Castle Wars case), and (b) consider resuming at the kill step rather than
   step 1 (see finding 3).
2. **NPC targets never get the clog-book marker.** `GuidanceTargetMarker` is instantiated
   only by `ObjectHighlightOverlay` and `GroundItemHighlightOverlay`; the NPC highlight path
   draws the outline but no marker (#720 promised the marker "floating above target").
   Operator-observed on Giant Mole and (absence) on Lanthus.
3. **Boss kill steps are not area-confirmable, stranding mid-room activations.** Giant Mole
   kill step has worldX/Y (1759,5189) but no `completionDistance`/`completionZone`; the
   state-derived start only confirmed it within the small default radius (fired at 13:46:03
   near the mole, not at 13:51:07 after she burrowed away → operator stuck on step 1/3 while
   in the lair). Data fix: explicit `completionDistance` (or lair zone) on ACTOR_DEATH steps;
   audit the corpus for the same gap.
4. **CLH writes the RSN to client.log** (T0.7 FAIL above): redact the player name from
   `EfficiencyCalculator` / `CollectionLogNetImporter` / `TempleOsrsKcSyncer` /
   `TempleSyncOrchestrator` / `PluginDataManager` log lines.
5. **Castle Wars Lanthus npcId is wrong: 1295 → 11650.** Cache receipt: abextm cache says
   npcId 1295 = "Mort'ton local"; Lanthus = 11650. Operator-observed: no glow on Lanthus.
   Follow-up: corpus-wide npcId/objectId cross-check against the cache.
6. **Entrance-object highlight only lives in a proximity "sweet spot".** The smoky-cave
   entrance step completes at r=10 on the surface, so the glow vanishes as you approach and
   the next (underground) step becomes current while you are still outside. Operator request:
   keep the entrance highlighted until the player actually transitions (complete on the
   underground side / plane change), the Shades-style pattern.
